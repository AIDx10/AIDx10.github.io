Aid Extend is a group of volunteers based in Kyiv, London, and Berlin. We are a small but mighty team of creative professionals, each hailing from diverse backgrounds, such as music, design, and social activism. Our mission is clear: to fight for sovereignty and freedom of Ukraine.

AIDx10 is a product of the people coming together to support one another. We aim to build support networks in creative communities - music, art, design, fashion - to amplify and multiply the frontline aid efforts and collective resilience against the terrorist state Russia.
